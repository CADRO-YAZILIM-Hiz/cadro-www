HR GLOSSARY

HR Glossary: Key Terms and Definitions

A comprehensive glossary of human resources terms and abbreviations every HR professional should know.

ATS (Applicant Tracking System)

Software that digitizes the recruitment process, managing applications and candidate flow in a centralized structure.

OKR (Objectives & Key Results)

A goal-setting methodology that defines measurable key results to achieve strategic objectives.

Onboarding

The process of integrating a newly hired employee into the company through orientation, training, and task assignments.

Offboarding

The structured process of managing an employee's departure, including exit interviews, asset returns, and knowledge transfer.

HRIS (HR Information System)

A software system used to collect, store, and manage employee data, payroll, benefits, and compliance.

KPI (Key Performance Indicator)

A measurable value that demonstrates how effectively an employee or department is achieving key business objectives.

360-Degree Feedback

A performance evaluation method where an employee receives feedback from supervisors, peers, subordinates, and sometimes clients.

Employee Engagement

The level of emotional commitment and involvement an employee has toward their organization and its goals.

Turnover Rate

The percentage of employees who leave an organization during a specific period. High turnover indicates potential workplace issues.

Compliance

Adhering to laws, regulations, and internal policies related to employment, data protection, and workplace safety.

Competency Matrix

A framework that maps employee skills and competencies against role requirements for development planning.

Time & Attendance

Systems and processes for tracking employee working hours, shifts, overtime, and absences.
