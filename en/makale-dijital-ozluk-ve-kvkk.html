Digital Employee Dossier and Data Protection Compliance: What You Need to Know Before Inspectors Arrive

As companies transition to digital employee records, questions about legal validity, data retention periods, and compliance requirements multiply. This guide covers the essentials.

Legal Validity of Digital Archives

Digital copies of employee documents are legally valid for internal management and audit purposes, provided they meet encryption and access control standards. However, original documents may still be required for certain legal proceedings.

Data Retention and Deletion Rules

Employee data must be retained for legally mandated periods, then securely deleted. CADRO automates retention scheduling and provides audit trails for every deletion event.
