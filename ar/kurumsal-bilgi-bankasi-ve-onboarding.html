FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the knowledge base differ from shared drives?

Unlike shared drives, CADRO's knowledge base includes version control, read receipts, targeted distribution, and searchable categorization.

Can onboarding tasks be customized per department?

Yes. You can create different onboarding checklist templates for each department, role, or location.

Does the system track policy acknowledgements?

Yes. Every published policy shows who has read it, who has acknowledged it, and who still needs to act.
