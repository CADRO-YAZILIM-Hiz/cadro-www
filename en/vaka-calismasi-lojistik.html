Logistics: Improving Location-Based Attendance and Workforce Control

Field-based operations require accurate location control and reliable attendance data. This case study shows how CADRO HRMS supports logistics teams with location-based workforce tracking and more structured attendance workflows.

Challenge

  • Field team check-in and attendance data came from fragmented sources.
  • Location validation and workforce visibility could not be monitored centrally.
  • Distributed operations created manual workload around shifts and field oversight.

CADRO approach

  • Attendance workflows and location-based rules were brought together in a more centralized HRMS.
  • Check-ins, field team visibility, and time control became clearer.
  • Operations and HR teams gained stronger oversight of field workforce movement.

Outcome

The company achieved more accurate location-based records, stronger attendance visibility, and more structured field operations. The result was more reliable workforce data and better operational control.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is geofencing attendance software used in logistics?

It helps ensure field teams check in and out from the right location, improving attendance accuracy.

What benefits do QR check-in and GPS validation provide?

They improve location accuracy, operational trust, and centralized workforce visibility.

Why is attendance tracking harder for field teams?

Because distributed teams, multiple locations, and high mobility make tracking more complex.
