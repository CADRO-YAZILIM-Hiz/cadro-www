PERFORMANCE MODULE

From OKRs to 360° feedback to AI analysis — a complete cycle

OKR Goal Management

Define goals at company, team, and individual levels with title, description, and due date. Progress is tracked 0–100%; status shows as TODO / IN_PROGRESS / COMPLETED in real time.

360° Review

Ratings (1–5 stars) are gathered from 4 sources: manager, peer, self-assessment, and subordinate. Comments and overall verdict accumulate on the same card per review period.

AI Talent Analysis

Collected review data is sent to Gemini AI. The system automatically summarizes an employee’s strengths, development areas, and career potential.

Period-Based Reporting

Each review period (e.g. “2025 Year-End”) is closed and archived separately. Historical comparison makes an employee’s growth curve visible.

Employee Selection & Comparison

Managers can review performance profiles of different employees side by side. Promotion and development decisions are made on data, not opinions.

Career & Promotion Planning

Score and comment data feeds into career equity analysis. High-potential profiles and at-risk talent are identified early.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an OKR and how is it different from KPIs?

An OKR (Objectives & Key Results) focuses on ambitious goals with measurable outcomes, while KPIs track ongoing performance metrics. CADRO supports both.

Can we customize the performance evaluation form?

Yes. You can create custom competency matrices, scoring scales, and evaluation questions for each role or department.

Does CADRO support 360-degree feedback?

Yes. Evaluations can include self-assessment, manager review, peer feedback, and direct-report input in a single cycle.
