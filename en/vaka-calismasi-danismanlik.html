Consulting: Improving Performance Management and Talent Visibility

Consulting firms rely on measurable goals, structured feedback, and talent visibility. This case study explains how CADRO HRMS supports performance management with OKR tracking, review workflows, and stronger talent insights.

Challenge

  • Goal tracking, managerial reviews, and employee development were spread across separate tools.
  • Feedback processes and talent decisions were not connected strongly enough, reducing leadership visibility.
  • Performance conversations were too fragmented to support a clear development picture.

CADRO approach

  • OKRs, 360 reviews, and managerial scoring were brought together in one performance management structure.
  • Strengths, development areas, and performance visibility became more holistic.
  • Leadership teams gained clearer data to support performance and talent decisions.

Outcome

With more measurable goals, clearer feedback loops, and stronger development visibility, performance management became more strategic. The result was better talent visibility and more refined decision support.

Because consulting firms depend on visible goals, structured feedback, and stronger talent decisions to maintain quality.

They combine objective tracking with broader feedback, creating a more complete view of employee performance.

What does talent visibility give managers?

It gives managers better clarity on strengths, development areas, and future potential profiles.
