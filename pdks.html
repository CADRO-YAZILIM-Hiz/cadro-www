COMPREHENSIVE GUIDE

Employee Attendance Tracking Software (PDKS)

PDKS (Personnel Attendance Control System) electronically records employee punch-in/out times, absences, late arrivals, overtime, and shift schedules. Explore CADRO's PDKS and payroll module below.

What does it do?

Collects employee attendance data, generates payroll reports, and automatically transfers data to the payroll system — replacing manual attendance sheets.

Who uses it?

HR teams, shift managers, and operations leaders in manufacturing, logistics, retail, and multi-location companies.

What to look for?

Mobile app support, GPS verification, shift planning, payroll integration, and multi-location management are the key criteria.

CADRO PDKS & Attendance Module

CADRO's PDKS module is a cloud-based attendance control system that requires no hardware, works on all devices, and integrates with HR data and payroll.

Mobile Check-In

Employees punch in/out via the CADRO mobile app. GPS location verification is available for field workers.

QR Code Attendance

A QR code installed in the workplace instantly records the employee's attendance. No hardware cost.

Shift Management

Weekly and monthly shift schedules can be created, assigned to employees, and changes notified instantly.

Overtime Tracking

Overtime hours are automatically calculated based on punch-in/out data and sent to the approval workflow.

Multi-Location Tracking

Branch and field teams in different cities are centrally tracked from one platform.

Attendance Reports

Monthly working hours, absence, and late-arrival reports are downloadable in PDF and Excel formats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDKS data secure?

CADRO PDKS data is stored in Turkey's servers in a KVKK-compliant manner. Role-based access ensures only authorised personnel can access attendance data.

Is PDKS data used in payroll calculation?

Yes. In CADRO, PDKS attendance data is automatically transferred to the payroll module. Monthly working hours, overtime, and absence deductions are accounted for in payroll calculation.
