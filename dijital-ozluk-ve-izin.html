DOSSIER & LEAVE MODULE

From document archive to leave approval — full HR cycle

Categorized Document Archive

Every document is stored with category and type (ID, contract, health report, etc.). Mandatory documents are flagged with is_mandatory; APPROVED / PENDING / REJECTED status is visible in real time.

Expiry & Missing Document Alerts

Each document's expiry_date is monitored automatically. Documents about to expire trigger alerts; staff with missing mandatory documents appear instantly in the compliance list.

Leave Request & Role-Based Approval

Employee creates request → department manager → HR / ADMIN approval is triggered sequentially. Every step is logged; requests close as PENDING / APPROVED / REJECTED / CANCELLED.

Seniority-Based Leave Calculation

Entitlement is automatically determined by tenure (0–1, 1–5, 5–15, 15+ years). Carry-over is controlled by max_carry_over_days; used / pending / remaining balance is visible at all times.

Multiple Leave Types

Annual (ANNUAL), sick (SICK), excused (EXCUSED), and maternity (MATERNITY) leaves are tracked in separate balances. Each type routes through its own approval flow.

Audit & Compliance Log

Every document action creates a DocumentActionLog entry: who did what, previous / new status, timestamp. Ready for GDPR and labor inspector audits.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is digital archiving of employee documents legally valid?

Yes. Although physical filing is legally required, digital archiving is legal and recommended for audits.

Can we import existing leave balances from spreadsheets?

Yes. During setup, you can import historical leave balances via an Excel template in seconds.

Does the system send automatic reminders for expiring documents?

Yes. CADRO alerts both the HR team and the relevant employee before expiration dates.
